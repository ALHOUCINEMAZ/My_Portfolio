Sales Analytics Dashboard

Role: Full-stack dev & data analyst · 2025 · Tools: PostgreSQL, Airflow, Next.js, React, FastAPI

End-to-end data pipeline (PostgreSQL + Airflow) feeding a Next.js dashboard; reduced manual reporting by ~80%.

Problem

Reporting was manual, siloed, and slow. Stakeholders lacked a single source of truth to monitor sales KPIs in near real-time.

Process

Built an ETL with Airflow to aggregate and clean data from transactional systems into PostgreSQL, then exposed APIs with FastAPI consumed by a Next.js dashboard.

Outcome

Automated reporting and interactive dashboards reduced time-to-insight and manual work by ~80%, improving decision cycles.

Highlights

  • End-to-end pipeline with testable DAGs
  • FastAPI endpoints with pagination and caching
  • Responsive Next.js dashboard with charting
